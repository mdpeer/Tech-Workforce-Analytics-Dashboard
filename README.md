# 📊 Tech Workforce Analytics Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard built to analyze workforce data and provide HR teams with actionable insights into employee demographics, attrition, salaries, and workforce distribution.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes workforce data from multiple technology companies and presents key HR metrics through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

The dashboard enables HR managers and business leaders to monitor employee trends, analyze attrition, compare departments, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Managing thousands of employee records manually makes it difficult to identify workforce trends and performance.

The objective of this dashboard is to provide HR teams with a centralized view of important workforce metrics.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze employee distribution
- Monitor attrition rate
- Compare departments
- Understand salary distribution
- Analyze employee demographics
- Support HR decision making

## 💡 Key Insights

- Total workforce consists of **10,000 employees**.
- Overall attrition rate is **33.77%**.
- Average employee age is **41.16 years**.
- Average salary is **120.17K**.
- Employees are distributed across multiple companies including Amazon, Cognizant, Deloitte, TCS, and Zoho.
- HR department has the highest employee count.
- Workforce is almost equally distributed across Remote, Onsite, and Hybrid work modes.
- Employees aged **46+** represent the largest age group.
